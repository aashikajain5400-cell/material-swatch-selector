# 📖 Project Overview

The **Material Swatch Selector** is a digital catalog management system developed as part of the **ProDesk Management Internship Sprint Project**.

The application replaces traditional paper records and spreadsheet-based material tracking with a centralized digital dashboard, enabling showroom staff to efficiently browse, organize, search, and manage material swatches.

The project focuses on improving operational efficiency while providing a modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface suitable for enterprise environments.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Furniture Builder currently manages material swatches using manual paper systems and Excel sheets, resulting in:

- Inefficient workflow
- Slow material searching
- Duplicate records
- High risk of data loss
- Manual inventory tracking
- Operational delays

These issues negatively impact productivity and day-to-day showroom operations.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

The Material Swatch Selector digitizes the entire workflow by providing a centralized application where staff can:

- Browse available material swatches
- Search materials instantly
- Filter by category
- Register new materials
- Delete outdated materials
- Preview detailed material information
- Manage showroom inventory digitally
